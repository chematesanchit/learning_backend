Life Hacks for Engineering Students

  • Keep Your Balance: When juggling circuits and equations, remember to balance studies with memes and social vibes. It's the ultimate engineering hack!
  • Cultural Coding: Explore hackathons and engineering clubs. Think of them as your virtual tech playground!
  • Stay Unique: Be as unique as your code! Don't just follow the code, write your own. Let your ideas fly high like debugging drones!
  • Memory Lane: Document your 'Eureka' moments! They'll serve as the blueprint for your engineering success story.
  • Engineering SOS: When a buddy's CPU is overheating with assignments, lend a hand, and say, "Ctrl + Alt + Support"! Share the debug love!
  • WIFI Wiz: Forgot the WiFi password? The command is like the 'secret formula' for engineers: netsh wlan show profile WiFi-name key=clear.
